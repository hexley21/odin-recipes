Khinkali: A Flavorful Georgian Dumpling Delight

Georgian dumplings filled with savory meat and spices, wrapped in thin dough, and boiled to perfection. A true delight for the taste buds, best enjoyed with friends and family. Symbolizing Georgian hospitality and culinary heritage.

Ingredients for 30 Khinkali:

  • 500g all-purpose flour
  • 250ml water
  • 300g ground beef or pork (or a mix)
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin (optional)

Instructions

  1. Make dough with flour and water, let rest for 30 mins.
  2. Mix meat, onion, oil, pepper, salt, cumin.
  3. Roll dough, add filling.
  4. Shape dumplings with pleats at the top.
  5. Boil in salted water for 10-12 mins.
  6. Serve hot, enjoy carefully!
