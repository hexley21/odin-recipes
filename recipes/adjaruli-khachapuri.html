Adjarian Khachapuri - Georgian Cheese-stuffed Delight

Indulge in the taste of Georgia with Adjarian Khachapuri, a delectable dish that embodies the country's rich cheese-making tradition.

Ingredients:

  • 500g all-purpose flour
  • 7g active dry yeast
  • 250ml warm milk
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 200g grated Georgian cheese (sulguni or imeruli)
  • 50g butter
  • 2 eggs

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix warm milk, sugar, and yeast. Let it sit for 5 minutes until it becomes frothy.
  2. Add flour and salt to the yeast mixture, knead the dough until smooth and elastic. Cover and let it rise for 1-2 hours.
  3. Preheat the oven to 220°C (425°F).
  4. Divide the dough into two portions. Roll one portion into an oval shape and fold the edges to create a boat-like structure.
  5. Fill the dough boat with half of the grated cheese and add half of the butter on top.
  6. Crack one egg in the center of the cheese and butter filling.
  7. Repeat steps 4 to 6 with the second portion of dough.
  8. Bake the Khachapuri in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes or until the crust turns golden and the cheese is bubbly.
  9. Remove from the oven and add an extra pat of butter on top.
  10. Serve the Adjarian Khachapuri hot and enjoy the oozy, cheesy goodness!
