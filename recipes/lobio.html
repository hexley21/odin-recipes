Lobio - Georgian Red Bean Stew

Prepare your taste buds for a journey to Georgia with Lobio, a flavorful and hearty red bean stew that embodies the country's love for wholesome ingredients and aromatic spices.

Ingredients:

  • 300g dried red kidney beans
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon ground fenugreek
  • 1 teaspoon ground paprika
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 tablespoon red wine vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro or parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Rinse the dried red kidney beans and soak them in water overnight.
  2. In a large pot, bring the soaked beans to a boil with enough water to cover them. Cook for about 1 hour or until the beans are tender. Drain and set aside.
  3. In the same pot, heat vegetable oil over medium heat. Add chopped onions and cook until they turn translucent.
  4. Stir in the minced garlic, tomato paste, ground coriander, fenugreek, and paprika. Cook for a few minutes until the mixture becomes fragrant.
  5. Add the cooked red kidney beans to the pot and mix well with the spiced onion mixture.
  6. Pour in enough water to cover the beans and add the bay leaf. Bring to a simmer and let it cook for another 10-15 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.
  7. Stir in the red wine vinegar and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Remove the bay leaf before serving.
  9. Garnish with fresh cilantro or parsley for a burst of freshness.
